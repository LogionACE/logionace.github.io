Privacy Policy

Effective: June 15, 2026

LogionOS, Inc. (operating as LogionACE) is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and what rights you have.

1. Information We Collect

We collect limited information through two channels:

  • Whitepaper download form — work email address and company name, provided voluntarily when you request a copy of our whitepaper.
  • Website analytics — anonymous, cookie-free analytics data such as page views, referral sources, and device type. We do not use tracking cookies or third-party advertising pixels.

2. How We Use Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Deliver the requested whitepaper to your email address.
  • Follow up regarding evaluation consulting, only when relevant to your expressed interest.
  • Improve our website, content, and services based on aggregated, anonymous usage patterns.

3. Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties for marketing purposes. Form submissions are processed through Formspree, Inc., a U.S.-based form handling service, solely for the purpose of delivering your submission to us. We may also disclose information when required by law, regulation, or valid legal process.

4. Data Retention

Personal information collected through our forms is retained for a maximum of 24 months. Evaluation logs and test artifacts are automatically purged after 90 days. You may request deletion of your data at any time by contacting us at the email below.

5. Data Security

We protect your data using industry-standard measures, including TLS encryption for data in transit and role-based access controls for stored data. No method of transmission or storage is 100% secure, but we take reasonable steps to safeguard your information.

6. International Transfers

Your data may be stored and processed in the United States. By using our website and services, you consent to the transfer of your information to the U.S., subject to applicable data protection laws.

7. Your Rights

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you.
  • Request correction of inaccurate data.
  • Request deletion of your data.
  • Restrict or object to certain processing activities.

These rights apply under regulations including the EU General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) and the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA). To exercise any of these rights, contact us using the information below.

8. Contact

For privacy-related inquiries or data requests:

privacy@logionace.com

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated effective date. We encourage you to review this page periodically.

Please also review our Terms of Service.
