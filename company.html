Company

Independent AI compliance evaluation.

LogionACE provides third-party evaluation report cards for AI systems. Operated by LogionOS, Inc., a Delaware-incorporated company focused on AI compliance infrastructure.

Mission

Make AI safer to adopt in regulated environments.

Enterprises need more than capability scores. They need evidence that AI systems behave safely under legal, regulatory, and operational constraints.

Position

Independent AI assurance.

LogionACE is designed as a lightweight, multi-jurisdictional, repeatable evaluation layer for AI system deployment risk.

Roadmap

Scaling the evaluation engine.

As evaluation data grows, we will continue to improve our proprietary testing infrastructure with expanded case coverage, published agreement benchmarks, and external audit.
