Evaluation independence

Why you can trust ACE results.

Proprietary evaluation engine

All evaluations are conducted through a purpose-built testing environment with deterministic checks, regulatory corpus grounding, and multi-pass verification.

Grounded scoring

Obligation cases are scored against an independently maintained regulatory corpus of 10,000+ rules — not model memory.

No pay-for-grade

Evaluation fees are fixed regardless of outcome. Payment does not influence scores. Results are determined by evidence, not engagement terms.
